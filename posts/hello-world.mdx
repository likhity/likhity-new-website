# Hello World

Welcome to this blog! This is a space where I'll share my thoughts on software engineering, AI, filmmaking, and whatever else catches my interest.

## Why a Blog?

I'm really inspired by [Ben Dicken's incredible posts for PlanetScale](https://planetscale.com/blog/io-devices-and-latency). I kinda want to do the same thing, just for fun. 

I want to start practice posting things on the internet more, write about stuff I'm learning about, and basically share my thoughts with the world.

## What to Expect

A mix of technical deep-dives into things I'm learning about, project write-ups, and occasional reflections. I may post about anything and everything (including my secondary interest in filmmaking).

Stay tuned.
